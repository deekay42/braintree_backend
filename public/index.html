Setup

Download desktop client & mobile app

League IQ consists of two parts: a desktop client and a mobile app. You need both. Android and iOS are both supported.

Register & Pair

Create an account in your League IQ mobile app & pair it with the desktop app: Scan the QR code on your desktop computer with your mobile app.

Start a game

Start your League IQ Desktop client and your League IQ mobile app. Then start a game of League of Legends. Recommendations will be displayed on your phone as well as the desktop app.

Get coached!

While in-game, drag your scoreboard(Press [Tab]) into this order: 1. Top, 2. Jungle, 3. Mid, 4. Bottom, 5. Support. To receive a recommendation on your phone press [Tab] + [F12], e.g. while channeling Back.

FAQ

Do I need both, the mobile app and the desktop app?

Yes. Since League of Legends does not allow overlays when it is in fullscreen, the desktop app is forced to stay in the background. To view your recommendations, simply use the app on your phone which mirrors the desktop app. If you have a second monitor however, you can have the desktop app running there.

How do the desktop and the mobile app differ?

They are more or less identical, however sign-ups are only supported in the mobile app.

It doesn't work. What do I do?

Try sending a test message by clicking on the "Send test message" button in the mobile app. If that doesn't work try pairing the phone again. You can also try entering the Settings menu in game and clicking 'Restore defaults' in the 'Interface' section. Restart the game as well as the app.

It still doesn't work.

Email the log file located at %LOCALAPPDATA%\LeagueIQ\log.txt to leagueiqapp@gmail.com and we will get back to you.
